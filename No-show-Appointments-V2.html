Introduction¶

The No-Show appointments dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. A number of characteristics about the patient are included in each row. The focus of this project will be

  • To identify the neighbourhood with most show of patients for appointments .
  • Calculate percentage of patients who show up for appointments receive scholarship and analyse the gender distribution among those who receive scholarship
  • To analyze the distribution of health conditions (Hypertension,diabetes, alcoholism, handicap) in both male and female patients who donot show up for appointments

Data Wrangling¶

General Data Properties¶

  • There are 110527 rows and 14 columns in the no show dataset.
  • The PatientID is in float and need to be converted to int
  • The data does not have any null values
  • The ScheduledDay column format has to be converted to DateTime
  • The AppointmentDay column format has to be converted to DateTime
  • The mean age of the patients in the data set is around 37. With max age of 115 and min age of -1 ( this can be a outlier needs to be verified)
  • There are no duplicate columns in the dataset
  • Columns "Gender, Scholarship, Hipertension, Diabetes, Alcoholism, SMS_received,No-show" each have 2 unique values.
    • No-show column has values 'No - if patient showd up for appoitnment' and 'Yes - if patient did not show up for appointment
    • Gender column has "M" for male and "F" for female
    • The rest of the columns have "0" and "1" values with 0 being false and 1 being True
  • From the data set we can infer that 79.80% of the patients showed up for their apointment and 20.19% of pateitns failed to show up for their appointments.

Conclusions¶

Research Question 1 = Which neighbourhood has the most show of patients. What percentage of patients who show up for appointments receive scholarship and what is the gender distribution among those who receive scholarship?¶

Findings :¶

  • The neighbourhood with the most number of patients who show up for the appointments is "JARDIM CAMBURI"
  • Only a very small percentage ~9% of people who show up for appointments receive scholarship.
  • Among patients who receive scholarship 6747 are Female and 1536 are male patients

Limitations :¶

  • The analysis answers the question as to which neighbourhood has most patient show up. Further analysing data based on gender and age can give more insights.
  • The number of patients who receive scholarship is very less and hence the scholarship feature has very less influence on the overall show and no-show of patients.

Findings :¶
