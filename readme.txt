
Investigate Dataset using python:
--------------------------------------

The No-Show appointments dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. A number of characteristics about the patient are included in each row. The focus of this project will be

1) To identify the neighbourhood with most show of patients for appointments .

2) Calculate percentage of patients who show up for appointments receive scholarship and analyse the gender distribution among those who receive scholarship

3) To analyze the distribution of health conditions (Hypertension,diabetes, alcoholism, handicap) in both male and female patients who donot show up for appointments
